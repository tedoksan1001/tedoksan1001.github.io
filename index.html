Expert Technical Rationale

The case for capital punishment is built on three pillars: Moral Desert, Public Safety, and Fiscal Realism. While opponents focus on procedural costs, the data shows that 'Life Without Parole' creates an unfunded geriatric healthcare liability costing taxpayers upwards of $100,000 per inmate annually. Furthermore, the 2025 University of Limerick study establishes a clear 7.6% deterrent effect, meaning abolition is a statistical choice to allow more homicides. When 63.8% of violent offenders recidivate, the only permanent guarantee for society is the ultimate penalty.
