Expert Technical Rationale

The case for capital punishment rests on Moral Proportionality and Fiscal Responsibility. While opponents cite procedural costs, they ignore the 'fiscal time bomb' of life sentences. Data from the 2025 University of Limerick study confirms a distinct deterrent effect, while USSC data proves that life sentences fail to neutralize the 63.8% risk of re-offense. True justice requires a punishment that reflects the finality of the crime and removes the ongoing burden of the criminal from the law-abiding public.
