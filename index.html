The "Life-Sentence Debt"

300%

Higher cost to house elderly "lifers" compared to average inmates due to advanced medical needs.

Technical Summary

Opponents focus on the procedural costs of the death penalty but ignore the unfunded liabilities of life imprisonment. In 2025, 63% of the "Life Without Parole" population is trending toward geriatric care, shifting the financial burden of heinous criminals onto the public healthcare budget.
