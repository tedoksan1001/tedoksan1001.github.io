Expert Technical Rationale

The argument for capital punishment is rooted in Moral Proportionality, Public Safety, and Economic Viability. Opponents frequently overlook the "fiscal time bomb" of life sentences; geriatric care for lifers costs taxpayers 300% more than standard housing. Furthermore, the 2025 University of Limerick study establishes a clear 7.6% deterrent effect. With a 63.8% recidivism rate for violent offenders, the death penalty remains the only definitive safeguard for the law-abiding public.
